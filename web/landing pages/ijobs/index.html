QR Based Digital Job Solution

We recognize that finding employment can be a tedious task. Technology and social media have become an integral part of the process; simply dropping off a CV is inefficient.

All of this can now be instant and seamless with iJobs.shop- a QR code based system joining the job seeker and employer. Merchants and businesses now have a simple way of receiving a candidate’s CV digitally. Merchants put up the iJobs unique sticker identifier on their windows, doors, trucks, and publishing to their websites and sharing through social media
