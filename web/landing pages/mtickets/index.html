Creat Your Own Events

Mtickets is an event operations managements and electronic ticketing platform. It helps in managing and tracking operations of all events, conferences, customers, exhibitors and organizers.

It tracks and communicates events operation management changes in real-time.It has multilingual support: English, Chinese, French and Spanish.

Why Mtickets Platform?

  • To Manange traffic flow of events and conference
  • To manange products and offers linked to events
  • To manange centralized information, documents, images and noted repository.
  • To manange venue space by creating booths in advance againts events
  • To track real time updates of events and conference schedule, customer
            activity and sales of tickets.
  • To reduce payment default by accepting payment on time.
  • To have better co-ordination among organizer, Exhibitor and Customer

Mticket Users

Admin

Manages various applications users, subscription packages, app content, all events and conference.

Organizer

Manages his/hers events, conference and exhibitor operations and creates multi-tier ticketing for the event.

Exhibitor

Views upcoming events and conferences and manages products and offers, purchases, event packages and ticket under it.

Customer

Views upcoming events and conference and buys event tickets and contact the organiser.

Benefits of Using Mtickets:

  • Save cost by saving time and streamlining operations
  • Track realtime updates of events and conference
  • Save human efforts by managing event communication
  • Increase communication among various stakeholders
  • Optimum data management because of centralised database.
  • Effective planning by estimating traffic flow of event
  • Quick and easy payment of subscriptions and tickets
  • Easily accessible on the go
