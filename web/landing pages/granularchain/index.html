How it works?

Our system can be used as the front end or the backend to any staging. The clients simply follow the registration process and digitally upload their documents. Then once documents have been received, they are verified and checked off as accepted or rejected, if they are rejected the consumer will be notified by SMS or email and would need to upload a replacement document.

Once all documents required have been accepted the client is sent an SMS two way verification to validate and time stamp that the documents and digital signature on file. All documents are now vaulted, and the KYC process is complete.
